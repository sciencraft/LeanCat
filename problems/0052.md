Theorem: In $\mathcal{S}\mathrm{et}$, $\mathcal{T}\mathrm{op}$, $\mathcal{G}\mathrm{rp}$, $\mathcal{A}\mathrm{b}$, $\mathcal{R}\mathrm{ing}$.
    Let $X,Y,Z$ be objects in one of these categories with morphisms $f:X\to Z$ and $g:Y\to Z$.
    Then $\{(x,y)\in X\times Y\mid f(x)=g(y)\}$ is the pullback $X\times_Z Y$ of $X$ and $Y$ over $Z$.
