Theorem: Let $\mathcal{F}\mathrm{rm}$ be the construct whose objects are frames, i.e. distributive suplattices, and whose morphisms are frame homomorphisms.
    Then there is a unique concrete functor $T:\mathcal{T}\mathrm{op}^{op}_0 \to \mathcal{F}\mathrm{rm}$ over $\mathcal{S}\mathrm{et}$, where $\mathcal{T}\mathrm{op}_0$ is the category of $T_0$ topological spaces.
